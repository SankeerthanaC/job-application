Our Goals

Our goal is to become one of the leading job agency in India

JON HUNT is the most innovative and largest online job agency in India. Founded in 2021, over the past time, JOB HUNT has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the agency is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

Our Work

JOB HUNT connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, JOB HUNT focuses on the entire career growth of candidates.

Our Passion

Our passion is to make job searching and employee hiring more convenient and easy.
