Read About Us

JOB HUNT is the most innovative and largest online job agency in India. Founded in 2021, over the past time, Job hunt has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the agency is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

JOB HUNT connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, job hunt focuses on the entire career growth of candidates.
